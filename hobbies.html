My hobbies

Basketball

I stand at 6'3 and weighs at 195 pounds, so I am a pretty big guy, which is exactly what everybody wants to be in the world of basketball.

I now play summer leagues with my highschool teammates. BTW, I can still dunk. Let's see if that's still the case when I'm 25.

Weight-lifting

Since highschool, alongside basketball, I've been into weightlifting. It takes up 2 hours a day, 4-5 days a week. You can imagine how packed my schedule is.

By sticking to that for about three years, with proper diet, I can now bench 265 pounds, or 120 kg as my one rep max. My goal is to beat my old man, which holds the record of 315 pounds, before I graduate.

Reading

Besides physical activities, I also like to sit down and enjoy a good read.

I'm a big fan of horror novels, and I would like to recomend "Devolution" by Max Brooks, the author of world war Z. Probably the most thrilling novel I ever read.
